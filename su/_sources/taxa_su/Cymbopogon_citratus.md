## Original description

*Cymbopogon citratus* was first described by **(DC.) Stapf** in:

> Bull. Misc. Inform. Kew 1906: 322 (1906)

This species was originally described as *Andropogon citratus* DC. in Cat. Pl. Horti Monsp.: 78 (1813), and was later reclassified to its current name.
