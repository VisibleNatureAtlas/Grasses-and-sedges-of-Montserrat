## Original description

*Lasiacis sorghoidea* was first described by **(Ham.) Hitchc. & Chase** in:

> Contr. U.S. Natl. Herb. 18: 338 (1917)

This species was originally described as *Panicum sorghoideum* Ham. in Prodr. Pl. Ind. Occid.: 10 (1825), and was later reclassified to its current name.
