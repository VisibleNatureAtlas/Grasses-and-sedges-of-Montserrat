About this atlas#

This atlas documents the grasses (Poaceae) and sedges (Cyperaceae) of Montserrat, a small British Overseas Territory in the Caribbean Lesser Antilles. Despite its modest size (102 km²), Montserrat harbours a rich diversity of native and introduced grass and sedge species.

This edition includes Wikipedia descriptions for 26 out of 25 species.
