## Original description

*Axonopus compressus* was first described by **(Sw.) P.Beauv.** in:

> Ess. Agrostogr.: 12 (1812)

This species was originally described as *Milium compressum* Sw. in Prodr. Veg. Ind. Occ.: 24 (1788), and was later reclassified to its current name.
